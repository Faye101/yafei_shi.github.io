Hi! I am **Yafei Shi (施雅菲)**, a Master's student in Technology Economics and Management at **Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE)**, advised by Prof. Shandong Mou.

My research focuses on **healthcare operations management**, **vehicle routing and scheduling**, and **supply chain resilience**. I develop optimization algorithms (ALNS, Gurobi, hybrid heuristics) for real-world logistics problems — from mobile clinic routing for elderly chronic disease screening to agricultural supply chain emergency response.

I am seeking **PhD opportunities starting Fall 2027**. Feel free to reach out if you see a match with your group.

---

## Research Interests

- **Healthcare logistics optimization**: Mobile clinic cooperative routing for chronic disease screening in rural elderly populations, incorporating demand decay and multi-vehicle synchronization
- **Operations research & optimization**: Adaptive Large Neighborhood Search (ALNS), mathematical programming, Gurobi, evolutionary game theory, system dynamics
- **Supply chain resilience**: Price prediction with machine learning (Random Forest, LSTM, GNN), emergency response mechanism design

## Projects

**Designing Proactive Healthcare Networks with Mobile Clinics for Co-morbidities** (Working Paper)
<br>*First Author*
- Modeled cooperative mobile clinic routing for rural elderly patients with multiple chronic conditions under demand decay and multi-vehicle synchronization.
- Designed an improved ALNS algorithm, outperforming Gurobi in both solution quality and computational efficiency.
- Presented at **INFORMS ICSS 2026** (Hangzhou) and **IFORS 2026** (Vienna).

**Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei Agricultural Supply Chain Resilience Coordination**
<br>*Team Leader*
- Built price prediction models using Random Forest, LSTM, and Graph Neural Networks (GNN); GNN achieved best performance across 1–5 day forecast horizons by capturing inter-product price dependencies.
- Developed evolutionary game theory model (21 variables) and system dynamics simulation; designed three-tier emergency response mechanism with ML-based early warning, distributed micro-warehouses, and mobile retail vehicles for last-mile supply assurance.

**JD Warehouse Storage Location Allocation and AGV Path Planning**
<br>*Team Leader*
- Built a multi-objective ABC storage allocation model and AGV dynamic programming model with clustering-based scheduling.
- Validated via Flexsim simulation on 20,000 SKUs; awarded **Second Prize in JD Logistics Cup** Smart Supply Chain Competition.
